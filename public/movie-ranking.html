The Best Movies According to Hieu

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

Arcane

Stunning animation. Captivating storytelling. Compelling characters. Absolute Cinema.

Frieren: Beyond Journey's End

Takes an amazing spin on normal animes: the story begins after the Demon King is defeated. You will be venturing with Fieren to explore how a thousand-year old elf started to appreciate life after just a short 10 years with her companions.

Inception

A very fast-paced, complex movie that asks you to question the reality of your life.
